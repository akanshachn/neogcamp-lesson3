Bits of my Career Journey

2023 - Changing career

Teaching in a government school was not the job my parents & I thought it would be! It was draining - emotionally, mentally and physically. I decided that I wanted to leave, but I had no idea what I would do if I left. My brother, who is a software engineer himself, encouraged me to try my hand at coding again. Over the course of the next few months, I started relearning how to code and convinced my parents to support my decision as well.

2019 - Teaching

Being a caretaker for my mother left me with a lot of time in between tasks. With my parents' encouragement, I started preparing for government teacher's exams, and cleared it in the first attempt. I joined a Delhi government school in November, 2019.

2019 - Leaving IT

In late 2018, I joined a full-time position in the same company where I interned. However, due to an accidental injury, my mother's health declined in March 2019 and I made the tough decision of quitting my job immediately, to take care of her.

2018 - Internship

It was during my first internship in the last semester of Post Graduation, that I truly come to love the world of coding. I learned a lot about the corporate world and work from a great team, met my best friend too!
